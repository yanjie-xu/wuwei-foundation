Applications may be considered in humanitarian relief, mental well-being, community resilience, animal welfare, wildlife rescue, and ecological compassion.

Submitting an application does not guarantee funding. Decisions depend on charitable alignment, feasibility, governance, transparency, available funds, and anticipated public benefit.
